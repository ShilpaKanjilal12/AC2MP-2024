Local Attractions

Bodh Gaya

Bodh Gaya is one of the most important pilgrimage sites for Buddhists as it is the place where Siddhartha Gautama, the Buddha, attained enlightenment under the Bodhi Tree.

Nalanda

Nalanda is renowned for its ancient Nalanda University, which was a center of learning and one of the earliest residential universities in the world.

Vaishali

Vaishali is an ancient city and holds historical and religious importance. It is believed to be the birthplace of Lord Mahavira, the founder of Jainism.

Golghar

Golghar, located in Patna, Bihar, is an iconic granary built by the British in 1786. Its unique beehive-shaped structure was designed to store grain and prevent famines, offering a panoramic view of the city from its top.
